The problem

Most portfolio trackers show you charts. They don't tell you what to do — and none of them factor in the tax bill when you sell. So people either hold losers too long, or sell winners weeks before they'd qualify for long-term capital gains rates and hand thousands to the IRS for no reason.

What TrimSmart does

  • Scores every holding on a 9-point quality framework (growth, margins, debt, valuation vs. analyst targets).
  • Flags what's weakening — positions failing quality checks and trading above fair value.
  • Times the tax — shows your gain as short-term vs. long-term, the tax cost of selling now, and how many days until a position turns long-term.
  • Suggests the least-taxable way to trim — specific-lot selling, loss harvesting pairs, and more.
